About

Astro-Link presents one of the most comprehensive Astrological content and reports on the web.


For centuries, turning our eyes to the starry sky has been a daily gesture, a habit that today is being lost and many, too many people, ignore it. almost the existence. It is certainly a cultural problem, but also light pollution plays its part: just think that about a third of humanity (including 60% of Europeans and 80% of North Americans) has never been able to enjoy one of the most beautiful natural shows: the Milky Way.


The Astrophile of the XXI century, besides cultivating a complex, but stimulating and challenging passion under the technical aspect and for the important contribution that it can still give to scientific research, therefore more and more remains the depositary of a knowledge and a practice as old as man, with the fundamental mission of preserving, transmitting and sharing it as much as possible with a society increasingly distracted but at the same time more and more in need of That’s why I’m here.


Astro-Link is born from the desire to spread and make known to ordinary people the beauty of Astronomy.Probing the universe opens the infinite wonders: stars, planets, exoplanets, galaxies, black holes, pulsars, brown dwarfs, nebulae, star clusters, extreme phenomena such as fast radio flashes, gravitational waves, gamma flashes. Advances in technology and the use of increasingly powerful telescopes, in optical, infrared, radio, ultraviolet, X-rays, gamma rays, have allowed astronomy to evolve into an increasingly surprising science, that allows you to take a look at the boundaries of the universe.


Our team hand-craft all of the content on the site, and we are constantly working together to conceptualize exciting new content features. Astro-Link's popular content offer currently covers multiple topics.
